# Pratiche

> *Italian for "practices". What I choose to cultivate regularly — not a habit tracker, a
> declaration of what I'm tending. `/senso-tracking` checks these against what the dailies
> actually show.*

| Practice | Intended frequency |
|----------|--------------------|
| Write (journal) | Daily |
| Move (walk, gym, anything) | 4× / week |
| Spanish study | 3× / week |
| One real conversation | Weekly |
| Phone off after 22:00 | Daily |

*The point isn't the streak. It's noticing the gap between what I say I cultivate and what I
actually live — and deciding, not drifting.*
