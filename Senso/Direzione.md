# Direzione

> *Italian for "direction". The most stable layer of Senso: who I want to be and where I want to
> go. It changes slowly. Everything else (the month, the week, the day) orbits around this.*

## Who I want to be

*A few sentences, first person. Not goals — a stance. Rewrite this only when something real shifts.*

Someone who builds things that outlast the week. Who rests on purpose, not from collapse. Who
keeps one or two relationships tended, not just maintained.

## Where I want to go

- Work that I choose, not work that chooses me.
- A body I can rely on — sleep, movement, food, in that order of leverage.
- Speak Spanish well enough to have a real conversation.

## Threads I keep coming back to

- Push vs rest — see [[Themes/Rest and Push]]
- Making, not just consuming.
