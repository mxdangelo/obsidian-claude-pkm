# Inbox

*Raw capture for the knowledge side. One item per line under `## To process` — a URL, an idea,
a clipping, a quick note. Feed it however you like — type here directly, or wire a clipboard
helper from your phone (gitignored, machine-local). Then run `/capture`: it routes each line —
a URL becomes a note in `Sources/` (or a bookmark in `Useful Links`), free text becomes a seed
in `Ideas/` or an entry in `Agenda`. The section empties as it's processed.*
